  1. Chill Like a Cucumber, Not an Onion: Stick that onion in the freezer and let it contemplate its life choices for 15 minutes. It'll be too busy shivering to make you cry. If only therapy was this easy for vegetables.
  2. Knife Sharp Enough to Slice Silence: Your knife should be so sharp it could cut through a politician's promises. A dull blade is about as helpful as a screen door on a submarine when it comes to onion-slicing.
  3. Onion Surgery: We're about to perform some veggie surgery here. Slice off the onion's extremities and strip it down to its inner layers. Think of it as giving the onion a makeover before we chop it to bits.
  4. The Slice and Dice Disco: Chop horizontally, then vertically, and finally, take it straight down for the party of the year. Your onion will be the star of the culinary dance floor.
  5. Airflow Engineering: Get your DIY skills on point by setting up a fan to blow those onion fumes away. Bonus points if you decorate it with some onion-shaped streamers.
  6. Zen Onion Meditation: Inhale through your nose, exhale through your mouth. Pretend you're at a yoga class, not a battle with an onion. Namaste, my aromatic friend.
  7. The Splash Zone: Slice onions underwater to create your own mini onion water park. It's the only place where you'll see onions take a dive without shedding a tear.
